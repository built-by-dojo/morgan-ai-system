# First Week Onboarding Playbook

Your AI team learns faster when you use it consistently. One task per day for 7 days — each teaches a new capability.

---

## Day 1: Get Oriented
**Goal:** Make sure everything works and your Director knows you.

Open Terminal, navigate to your project, and type `claude`. Then say:

> "Hi, I'm [your name]. Tell me what you know about me so far, and what agents are on my team."

Then tell it 3 things about your business that matter most right now.

**Why this matters:** Your Director starts building your founder profile from day one. The more it knows, the smarter it gets.

---

## Day 2: Meeting Prep
**Goal:** See how your EA prepares you for meetings.

Before your next meeting, tell the Director:

> "I have a meeting with [person] about [topic] in [timeframe]. Help me prepare."

The Director will route this to your EA, which will create a brief with context, talking points, and open questions.

**Why this matters:** Meeting prep is the highest-ROI daily habit. 5 minutes of AI prep saves 30 minutes of scrambling.

---

## Day 3: Post-Meeting Capture
**Goal:** Turn meetings into permanent knowledge.

After any meeting, brain-dump to your Director:

> "Just finished my call with [person]. Here's what happened: [stream of consciousness about the meeting]"

Your team will extract action items, update contact profiles, and flag follow-ups.

**Why this matters:** Most meeting value is lost within 24 hours. This captures it permanently.

---

## Day 4: Research Request
**Goal:** Test your Specialist's domain expertise.

Ask your Director a strategic question about your industry:

> "What are the top 3 trends in [your industry] that I should be paying attention to right now?"

Or:

> "Research [competitor/topic] and give me the key takeaways I can act on this week."

**Why this matters:** Your Specialist saves hours of research time and surfaces insights you'd miss.

---

## Day 5: Thinking Partner
**Goal:** Use your Director as a strategic sounding board.

Bring a real business decision you're wrestling with:

> "I'm trying to decide between [option A] and [option B]. Here's the context: [explain]. Help me think through this."

**Why this matters:** The Director combines everything it knows about you, your business, and your industry to give genuinely useful perspective. This is where the system compounds.

---

## Day 6: System Check
**Goal:** Make sure your vault is growing and your system is healthy.

Ask the Dev agent:

> "Show me what's in my knowledge vault. How many files? What's been saved this week?"

Then ask:

> "Is the health beacon running? When was the last successful report?"

**Why this matters:** A healthy system = a system that keeps getting smarter. This builds the habit of checking.

---

## Day 7: The Magic Moment
**Goal:** See what your AI team has learned about you in one week.

Ask your Director:

> "What have you learned about me this week? What patterns do you see? What would you recommend I do differently?"

This is the moment where the system goes from "cool tool" to "I can't work without this."

**Why this matters:** The Founder Intelligence Engine needs data to work. After 7 days of consistent use, it has enough to start making genuinely insightful observations.

---

## After Week 1

By now you should be in the habit of:
- Morning: "What do I need to know today?"
- Before calls: "Prep me for [meeting]"
- After calls: "Here's what happened: [brain dump]"
- Decisions: Think out loud with your Director
- Fridays: "What patterns did you notice this week?"

The system gets exponentially better with consistent use. Week 1 is good. Month 1 is great. Month 3 is transformative.
